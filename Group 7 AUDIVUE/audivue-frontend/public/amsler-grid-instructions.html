Amsler Grid Test Instructions

    1. Wear your reading glasses if you normally use them
    2. Cover one eye
    3. Focus on the central dot of the grid
    4. Check if any lines appear wavy, distorted, or missing
    5. Select whether you see distortion or not
    6. Repeat for the other eye

This test helps detect vision problems affecting your central vision, such as macular degeneration.
