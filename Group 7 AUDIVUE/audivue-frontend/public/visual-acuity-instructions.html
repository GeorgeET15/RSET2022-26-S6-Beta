Visual Acuity Test Instructions

    1.Sit at least 6 meters away from the screen
    2.Cover one eye with your hand
    3.Read the smallest line of letters/numbers you can see clearly
    4.Type the letters/numbers you see in the response box
    5.Repeat for the other eye if needed

This test helps assess your visual acuity, which measures how clearly you can see details at a distance.
